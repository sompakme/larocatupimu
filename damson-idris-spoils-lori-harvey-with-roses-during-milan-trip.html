Damson Idris Spoils Lori Harvey With Roses During Milan Trip

You know what they say – absence makes the heart grow fonder. That certainly seems to be the case for Damson Idris and Lori Harvey, who have spent a significant amount of time apart this month due to their respective commitments. Being apart from your partner is hard, but luckily, the lovers were able to cross a major relationship milestone together recently – their red carpet debut. At the premiere of the actor's Snowfall series' sixth season, he was joined by the model, who looked as radiant as ever.

Social media users couldn't dispute the hair's obvious good looks. What they did argue, however, was the chemistry between the pair, which some say is seemingly non-existence. In response to the hate comments, Harvey clapped back on Instagram. She said that her distant body language was to protect her man's expensive suit from the makeup on her skin, and over the weekend, she proved how thoughtful of a boyfriend he can be.

Damson Tears Up Trinidad's Carnival

For his part, Idris took a trip to the islands of Trinidad and Tobago this February to celebrate the nation's famous Carnival. Nicki Minaj was also in attendance for this year's events and even gave her first performance in such time, much to patron's pleasure. The English star clearly enjoyed his vacation and partying, as he chiefly declared on Instagram that he wants to buy a home there ASAP.

"I need a Trini real talk, 'bout to buy a crib here," he told his followers. "Even the air taste good," Idris noted, after markedly praising Trinidad's delicious food and drinks. His only problems, it seems, are the mosquitos who "treat [him] like Popeyes," and the lack of his girlfriend's presence.

Lori Takes Milan Fashion Week

On the other side of the world, Harvey spent this past weekend in Milan, Italy. Like many others, she was invited to show support at a designer runway. Creative Director Rhuigi Villaseñor requested the Memphis native's presence at his presentation, where she sat in the front row alongside a slew of celebrities. DDG was present (following his appearance at Gucci's F/W '23 collection with Halle Bailey), as was TikTok sensation Dixie D'Amelio.

While he couldn't join her on the trip, Idris made sure to keep his girl smiling with a romantic delivery during her travels. On her IG Story, Harvey posted a huge collection of red roses, as well as a note reading, "I love and miss you." In barely visible text near the bottom, she wrote, "My man, my man, my mannn," tagging her beau to show him some love.
