What is tongue thrust?

published on October 02, 2024
Tongue thrusting is the habit of pushing your tongue forward between the upper and lower teeth when you swallow. The proper positioning for the tongue is for the tip to push against the gum above the back of your upper front teeth. What is the cause of tongue thrust? Causes of Tongue ThrustTongue thrust is an orofacial myofunctional disorder that typically is caused by thumb-sucking or extended pacifier usage. It also may be due to an untreated tongue-tie.
