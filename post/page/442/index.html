'Seinfeld,' 'Close Encounters of the Third Kind' Actor Was 86

Dynarski died Feb. 27 in a rehabilitation center in Studio City, playwright Ernest Kearney announced. The Brooklyn native also worked twice on the original Star Trek, as the miner Ben Childress on the 1966 episode “Mudd’s Women” and as Krodak, who represents a city up for Federation membership, on the 1969 installment “The Mark of Gideon.
