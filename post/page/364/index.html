Can I use white Teflon tape for propane?

published on July 18, 2024
Answer It is not recommended to use plumber’s Teflon tape on gas pipe fittings because the tape will deteriorate with time and allow gas vapours to escape through the fitting. It is white in colour and thinner than the yellow gas-rated Teflon tape that is used by plumbers. Gas leaks from pipelines and connections are very hazardous and may result in an explosion.
