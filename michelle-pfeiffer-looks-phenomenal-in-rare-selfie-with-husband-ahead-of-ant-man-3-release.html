Michelle and David have been married for 29 years

    star Michelle Pfieffer has shared a rare picture of her husband, TV producer David E. Kelley. The two have been married for 29 years and although they keep their relationship out of the spotlight, Michelle shared the selfie to her 2.7 million followers to honor their love for Valentine's Day.
