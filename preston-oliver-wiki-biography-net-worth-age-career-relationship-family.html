Known for his remarkable acting skills, Preston rose to prominence with his lead role as ‘Griffin’ in Disney’s TV series ‘Secrets of Sulphur Springs.’ With a promising career ahead, Preston has already showcased his talent in various shows and commercials.

Career

Preston Oliver’s acting journey began when he participated in a school drama during his early years. As his passion for acting grew, he joined acting classes and eventually connected with ‘Coast to Coast Talent Group’ to further enhance his skills. With determination and hard work, Preston made his acting debut as Riley Boyd in NBC’s ‘Chicago Fire.’ He also appeared in ‘This Is Us’ and secured a commercial for DISH. In 2020, he starred in the science fiction drama series ‘Roswell, New Mexico’ as young Michael. However, his breakthrough role came in 2021 when he landed the lead role of ‘Griffin’ in Disney’s ‘Secrets of Sulphur Springs,’ which marked a significant milestone in his career.
