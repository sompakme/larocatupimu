Watch Ryback Review A Little Bit Of The Bubbly

Chris Jericho uttered a phrase after winning the AEW World Title that has gone on to have a life of its own. A Little Bit Of The Bubbly soon took over and the merchandising possibilities followed.

After selling a ton of Little Bit Of The Bubbly t-shirts, Jericho moved on to putting out actual booze. With the held of Stephen Amell’s Nocking Point Wines, “A Little Bit Of The Bubbly” is now a reality.

It didn’t take Chris Jericho very long at all to sell 12,000 bottles. Now fans all over are enjoying A Little Bit Of The Bubbly for themselves. This includes Ryback.

The Big Guy recently uploaded a new installment of Ryback TV where he indulged in A Little Bit Of The Bubbly. After trying it out he had a pretty glowing review.

“I gotta say, I like it. Not too bad at all. Man, Chris is gonna make a lot of money on this.”

Obviously, A Little Bit Of The Bubbly has fans. Now that Ryback gave his stamp of approval it seems really hard to find an excuse not to buy a case of bottles ourselves.
