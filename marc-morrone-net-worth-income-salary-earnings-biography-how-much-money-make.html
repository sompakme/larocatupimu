Marc Morrone (born 1960 in the Bronx, New York) is an American animal trader and breeder who is also the presenter of the Mag Rack television show The Pet Shop with Marc Morrone. As a television presenter, he was the host of Petkeeping with Marc Morrone, a show produced by Martha Stewart Living Omnimedia that initially concluded its broadcast on September 17, 2006, but many stations throughout the nation are still airing old episodes to fulfil their E/I obligations. The Hallmark Channel has restarted production of the show, which was previously on hiatus. He now lives with his wife and a large number of dogs.
