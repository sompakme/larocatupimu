Keren Jane Woodward (born 2 April 1961 in Bristol, England) is a member of the British female group Bananarama, which she co-founded with her school friend Sara Dallin and Irish musician Siobhan Fahey. Dallin, her bandmate, has known her since they met at Bromley Heath elementary school when she was four years old. Rodway Secondary Modern School was her alma mater. She currently resides in Cornwall with her long-term partner, former Wham! singer and guitarist Andrew Ridgeley, and her son Thomas (born 1986) from a previous relationship with model David-Scott Evans. Woodward is a single man who has never married.
