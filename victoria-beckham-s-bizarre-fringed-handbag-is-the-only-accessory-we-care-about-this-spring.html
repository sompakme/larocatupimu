Victoria Beckham's bizarre fringed handbag is the only accessory we care about this spring

Victoria Beckham is not afraid to push the boundaries in the accessory department – that much we know for sure. Just a few days ago, her cult-adored chain pouch bag was at the forefront of our wish lists thanks to a hilarious video featuring her husband David.

In case you missed it, the footballing legend offered his hot fashion take by taking the mickey out of Victoria's earnest announcement that the bag was back in stock.

Another day, another killer VB accessory to sink our teeth into, and her latest is of the fringed variety.

The 48-year-old stepped out in London on Thursday at her eponymous label's Dover Street store for a very special event. Victoria was interviewed by art historian Katy Hessel about how art and colour inform and inspire her work on her clothing and beauty labels.

Victoria eschewed her usual all-black agenda in favour of a shade that had us doing a double take. The designer rarely branches out from her tried and tested dark colour palette, and so her pale pink ruched midi dress felt unexpected and fresh.

At first glance pastel hues may feel like a classic spring trope, but Victoria's approach felt anything but clichéd. Harnessing the power of drapery and exuding a certain balletic elegance, the designer looked picture perfect.

VB topped off her look with the most unconventional accessory: a fringed bag. The supercharged textural piece had an enchanting kinetic energy about it, featuring masses of lustrous cream-coloured tassels.

Accessory aficionados, we bear sad news. The one-of-a-kind clutch is not currently shoppable, although the moment it does become available to buy, we will be channelling David's overly enthusiastic "YES!" energy. (For what is marriage, if not a lifelong opportunity to take the mickey?)

Victoria topped off her look with white fishnet tights and single-strap peep-toe heels and wore her hair loose in a relaxed side parting.

A model walking the runway in Victoria Beckham's SS23 show 

Her entire ensemble came courtesy of her eponymous brand's SS23 line, first showcased on the runway last September in Victoria's Paris Fashion Week debut.
