Carolina Herrera Tango Shoes - Buy Carolina Herrera Tango Shoes

Carolina Herrera has a famed Andy Warhol portrait hanging in her Manhattan offices—proving the ultra-elegant designer knows of NYC nightlife in the '70s. In a nod to that glamorous era, Herrera designed a slim T-strap sandal, available in almost endless color ways for her latest CH collection. "Studio 54 was the world's most glamorous rendezvous," Herrera says, "now it is a legend." We can almost hear the disco beats from here. 

The CH Carolina Herrera Tango sandal is available now at all CH Carolina Herrera boutiques.
