Brooklyn Beckham dating Madison Beer

Brooklyn Beckham is reportedly dating American singer Madison Beer. Their relationship was seemingly confirmed when they were spotted kissing after a date at The Grove shopping mall in Los Angeles this weekend.

According to reports, the pair were also seen enjoying dinner with Victoria Beckham at Hollywood hotspot Catch on Saturday night.

The pair, who are both 18, were first photographed together in June at a Wonderland dinner hosted by Beer at The Ivy Soho Brasserie in London.

Brooklyn and Madison with Wonderland founder Huw Gwyther. Getty Images

Beer shot to fame when Justin Bieber tweeted a link to a YouTube video of the star singing. Shortly afterwards she was signed to Island records and has since amassed more than 9.6 million followers on Instagram.
