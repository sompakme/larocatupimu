WWEs Current Plan For Emma After Her Unexpected Return

Emma recently made her shocking return to WWE as Ronda Rousey’s mystery opponent on SmackDown. Despite putting up a valiant effort against Ronda Rousey, Emma came short and suffered a loss. After the match, many fans were speculating if this was a one-off appearance or if Emma was back in WWE.

Click here for our complete coverage for WWE SmackDown this week.

Now, it looks like WWE has finally provided a confirmation regarding Emma’s status. WWE has officially placed Emma back on their website following her SmackDown match against Ronda Rousey.

This means that Emma is going to be part of SmackDown going forward. Emma also tweeted regarding the same by saying she was home.

“Home [heart emoji],” tweeted Emma.

It will be interesting to see how WWE uses Emma this time. Her first two runs with the company were underwhelming. However, now with a new regime, things may be different for her going forward.
