Ex Titan Games Star Ivy Nile Debuts On WWE NXT 2.0

WWE took the opportunity to debut a few Superstars to the NXT brand during the season premiere of NXT 2.0. The Diamond Mine are still a dominate faction, and now they have a new member.

Click here for our complete coverage of WWE NXT 2.0

Ivy Nile was introduced during the show as a part of the Diamond Mine faction. Her real name is Emily Andzulis and she was a part of The Rock’s Titan Games.

This is a big addition to NXT television and now she will be in the mix as part of the Diamond Mine. She didn’t have a match, but Malcolm Bivens really talked her up on the microphone.
