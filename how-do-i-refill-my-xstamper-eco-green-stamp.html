What is the process for unlocking a stamp?

1- Lightly press the top of the stamp to line the pad with the opening at the back of the stamp. 2- Press the bottom of the stamp to align the pad with the aperture at the rear of the stamp. 2- Using the lock/unlock button, secure the stamp in place. 3- After removing the lid, remove the old pad and replace it with the new pad. 4- Press the lock/unlock button to unlock the stamp.
