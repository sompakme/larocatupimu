Moisturising products to combat cold weather

Four of the top products to try

    Whether winter is settling in where you live, or you're heading to a cold Christmas market this festive season, you need to make sure you have the perfect moisturiser to keep your face in tip top condition. Not sure what products to invest in? Here are a few moisturising cream suggestions that have been highly recommended for combating cold weather.

    Kiehl's Ultra Facial Cream

    A brand famous for its nourishing products, this replenishing moisturiser by Kiehl's is like butter on your skin. It's lightweight and rubs in easily and the quick absorption leaves your complexion smooth, silky and hydrated almost instantly. The ingredient Imperata Cylindrical is known to retain water, so your skin will stay hydrated even against the coldest winds that can dry your face out.
