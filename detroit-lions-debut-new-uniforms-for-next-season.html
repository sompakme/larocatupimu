Detroit Lions Debut New Uniforms For Next Season

The Detroit Lions have officially unveiled their new uniforms for next season, featuring four different styles including a throwback jersey that features a blue top and grey pants.

The Lions' home jersey will look similar to those throw backs, while their road jerseys pair a white jersey with blue pants. Lastly, the Lions also unveiled their "Color Rush" jersey which features a monochromatic "chrome" (read: grey) finish.
