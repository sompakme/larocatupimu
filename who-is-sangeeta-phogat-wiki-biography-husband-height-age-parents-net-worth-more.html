Who is Sangeeta Phogat? Wiki, Biography, Husband, Height, Age, Parents, Net worth &amp; More

Sangeeta Phogat (born on 05 March 1998; Age: 23 years old) is a well-known wrestler, international player, fitness freak, social media star, and fitness coach from Haryana, India. She is famously known as the younger sister of a popular freestyle wrestler and Olympic athlete named Geeta Phogat. 

Geeta has played several matches for the country and has also won medals in Commonwealth Games. Apart from this, Sangeeta has also played several wrestling matches at the World levels. She takes wrestling training along with her sister Ritu Phogat from her father. 

Let me tell you that Sangeeta’s father Mahavir Singh Phogat is a renowned wrestling coach and former wrestler. Moreover, Sangeeta’s husband Bajrang Punia also won the bronze medal in Tokyo Olympics 2020 in wrestling. 

Sangeeta Phogat’s Biography & Birthday 

Phogat took birth on March 05, 1998, in village Balali, Bhiwani district, Haryana, India. She is currently living in Jhajjar, Haryana, India. She is 23 years old as of 2021.

Phogat Sisters
