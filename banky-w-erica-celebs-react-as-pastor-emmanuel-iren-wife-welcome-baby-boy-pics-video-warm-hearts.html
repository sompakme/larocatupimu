Banky W, Erica, Celebs React As Pastor Emmanuel Iren, Wife Welcome Baby Boy, Pics, Video Warm Hearts

  • Pastor Emmanuel Iren and his wife Laju have welcomed a bouncing baby boy as the cleric shared adorable pictures and a video online
  • A video from the baby gender reveal party saw Pastor Iren kicking a ball into the net as a blue-coloured smoke filled the air
  • Popular celebrities like Banky W and BBNaija's Erica, among others, have taken to social media to congratulate the couple

Popular preacher Pastor Emmanuel Iren of Celebration Church International (CCI), and his wife, Laju, announced the birth of their third child, a baby boy, on Saturday, September 7.

Iren and Laju took to their social media accounts to share the delightful news.

Pastor Iren, who shared beautiful maternity photos, also put up a video from their gender reveal celebration.

A clip showed the cleric kicking a ball into the net as a blue-coloured smoke filled the air.
