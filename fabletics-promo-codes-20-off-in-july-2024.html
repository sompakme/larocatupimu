FAQs

Does Fabletics offer free shipping?

Yes! Free standard shipping is available at Fabletics on orders over $49.95. If you would like your order even faster, there is a charge of $15.95 for express shipping. VIP members can enjoy free shipping on every Fabletics order. 

Does Fabletics accept returns?

Fabletics have a 45-day returns policy so, should you not be totally satisfied, you have plenty of time to make a return. Returns aren’t free at Fabletics, there is a fee of $6.95. You can find out all you need to know about making a return on the Fabletics website. 

Does Fabletics have a membership program?

Yes - customers can sign up for the Fabletics Flexible VIP Membership Program for $59.95 per month and receive a wealth of benefits, including 20-50% off everything, free shipping on orders over $49.95, free access to the Fabletics FIT app, and loads more.  

How can I earn Fabletics rewards?

VIP members earn 2 points for every dollar spent on purchases bought using the Fabletics BeneFit Loyalty Program.

Does Fabletics have a refer-a-friend program?

Yes - customers who refer a friend to Fabletics will receive a $10 voucher towards their next purchase, and the friend will receive a discount on their first purchase too.
