Eileen Murray (born 1958) is an American financial services professional who now serves as co-President and Chief Operating Officer of Bridgewater Associates, where she was previously employed as a vice president. Morgan Stanley, Credit Suisse First Boston, Duff Capital Advisors, and Investment Risk Management are among the companies where Murray has worked as a senior executive. Her accomplishments have been recognised by U.S. Banker magazine, which named her one of the 25 Most Powerful Non-Bank Women in Banking for the years 2007 and 2008.
