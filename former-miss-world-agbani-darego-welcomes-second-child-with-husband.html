Former Miss World Agbani Darego welcomes second child with husband

- Agbani Darego Danjuma has confirmed the birth of her second child with her husband, Ishaya Danjuma

- The former Miss World and model, on December 4, took to her Instagram page to share the good news

- The beauty queen who got married to her long time partner in 2017, welcomed their first child in September 2018

- Agbani who was crowned Miss World in 2001 was the first indigenous African to win the title

Congratulations are in order for ex-beauty queen and model, Agbani Darego Danjuma as she recently became a mum for the second time.

On December 4, the former Miss World took to her official Instagram page to post the drawing of a Stork delivering a baby surrounded by baby-friendly items.

The proud mummy who already has a son with her husband, Ishaya Danjuma, captioned the cheerful painting with a simple 'Thankful'.
