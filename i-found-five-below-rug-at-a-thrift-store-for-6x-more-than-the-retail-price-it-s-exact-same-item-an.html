A RETAIL superfan has told how she was left stunned when she found a rug being sold at a thrift store six times more than its original price.

The content creator said the Five Below accessory was retailing for $30.

Five Below has been part of the budget retail scene for more than two decades and sells affordable items.

The retailer sells a whole range of items, including fashion accessories, room décor, and sports games.

TikToker Alexa Berghager (@alexaa.berghager) was left stunned when she came across the rug on sale for $30.

In the clip, she said: “No way, I just found the five below cherry rugs at a thrift store selling for $30!?!”

The 28-inch by 30-inch rug comes in the shape of a bunch of cherries.

Bargain hunters can pick up the rug for $5.55 on the Five Below website.

Five Below has gushed over the shape of the rug, describing it as a cool shape and said shoppers can cheer up their room decor.
