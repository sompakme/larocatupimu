Brent Faiyaz tour 2023: How can I get tickets?

UP-AND-COMING R&B star Brent Faiyaz announced in May 2023 that he will be embarking on a worldwide headline tour this summer.

The tour will kick off in the United States in July and then Faiyaz will travel to Canada and Europe to play multiple shows through November.

How can I get tickets for Brent Faiyaz's tour?

Faiyaz announced his upcoming tour on May 2, but fans will still have to wait a few more days before they can purchase tickets.

Presale is set to begin on May 3 and then tickets will be available to the general public starting May 5, at 10am CDT.

Tickets can be purchased directly on Faiyaz's website or on Ticketmaster.

Where will Brent Faiyaz be performing?

Faiyaz will kick off his "F*** the World, It's a Wasteland" tour on July 16, 2023, at the Broccoli City Festival in Washington, DC.

Who is Brent Faiyaz?

Christopher Brent Wood is an R&B singer who goes by the stage name Brent Faiyaz.

He was born on September 19, 1995, in Columbia, Maryland, and first rose to fame in the late 2010s after he was featured on GoldLink's 2016 single, Crew.

The song was certified 6x Platinum by the RIAA and earned him a Grammy Award nomination for Best Rap/Sung Collaboration.

The following year, Faiyaz released his debut studio album, Sonder Son, which featured the hit songs Gang Over Luv, Talk 2 U, and Stay Down.
