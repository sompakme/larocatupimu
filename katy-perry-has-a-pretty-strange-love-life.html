Katy Perry Has A Pretty Strange Love Life

Katy Perry reportedly began dating Orlando Bloom in January 2016, but a source close to the actor told People that he was "still keeping his options open" throughout his relationship Perry. It's unclear if they were on the same page about that. An insider told Radar Online that "Orlando never changed his ways with hanging out with other women [during the relationship]. He would still continue to hang out with his actor buddies and there were always a ton of sexy women around."

Bloom was even photographed in some compromising positions with other ladies before he and Perry announced their breakup. In May 2016, TMZ snapped Bloom hugging and allegedly kissing much-younger pop star Selena Gomez in Las Vegas, though witnesses later insisted that Gomez went back to her hotel room alone. On March 1, 2017, the Daily Mail published photos of Bloom cozying up to Erin McCabe, a daughter of Global Green President Les McCabe. Bloom reportedly rubbed Erin's backs and even swapped place cards with other guests at a gala to sit together. Six days later, he and Perry released a joint statement announcing they were dunzo ... at least for a while.
