Sundance Film Festival 2014: Inside InStyle's Portrait Studio!

Hello from Park City, Utah! InStyle is here at the Sundance Film Festival — smack in the middle of Main Street — catching up with all the stars debuting their new films at our pop-up portrait studio. Anna Kendrick dropped in to tell us about her roles in Happy Christmas, Life After Beth, and The Voices, Christina Hendricks promoted her new movie God's Pocket, Kristen Stewart popped by with the cast of Camp X-Ray, and Julianne Nicholson filled us in on her new Sundance Channel drama The Red Road. Flip through to see all the behind-the-scenes photos and keep checking InStyle.com to see more throughout the festival.
