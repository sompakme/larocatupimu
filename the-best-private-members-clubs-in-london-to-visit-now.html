The best private members clubs in London to visit now

Membership-only bars and clubs don't tend to crop up often in South London, which is what makes Upstairs At The Department Store in Brixton such an attractive proposition. The space, which sits atop the historic The Department Store building, offers a low-key, yet stylish decorated bar, rooftop terrace and a restaurant, serving seasonally changing lunch and dinner menu. By day, members are offered free yoga classes and creative workshops, and by night live music and DJ sets. There are also two private dining rooms, including a space underneath the property's beautiful glass dome, perfect for intimate events. The terrace, which offers blankets and heaters to keep visitors warm in the colder months, also boasts views of the city skyline. Perhaps most appealing of all, membership is very affordable.

Cost: Annual membership is £240, which can be paid in monthly installments of £23

Membership requirements: Applications can be made online, and no referrals are necessary.

Address: 10 Stockwell Avenue, SW9 8BQ
