    Finally, the first Monday in May has arrived, which for fashion and beauty fanatics, means the return of the Met Gala - one of the most illustrious events in the fashion calendar.

    Each year, the event, headed up by sartorial powerhouse Anna Wintour, serves as a dazzling stage where fashion's elite push the boundaries of creativity and extravagance. Among the shimmering gowns and avant-garde ensembles that adhere to the yearly themes and dress codes, the jewellery also takes centre stage, transforming the red carpet into a mesmerising display of glamour and sophistication. 

    See below the most dazzling jewellery moments from this year's event, themed with the dress code 'Garden of Time'...

    Cardi B

    Cardi is no stranger to a mesmerising red carpet moment... she paired her ginormous black gown with dramatic diamond and emerald jewellery from Kamyen Jewellery.

    Irina Shayk

    Irina paired her slinky silver maxi with a wide-brimmed choker and plenty of diamond bracelets from Swarovski.

    FKA Twigs

    The British singer-songwriter opted for showstopping earring cuffs by Panconesi that shrowded her ears.



     

    Cara Delevingne

    Cara D revived the Y2K-esque bracelet mega stacking style and gave it the most glamorous makeover, all of which were lab-grown diamonds by Vrai.

    Dua Lipa

    Dua paired her daring ensemble with a tennis-style necklace with a cluster pendant from Tiffany & Co.

    Taika Waititi

    The filmmaker and husband of Rita Ora wore the Royal Tourbillon watch and the Yellow Diamond Riviera necklace, both from Jacob & Co and together totalling almost $7.5 million.

    Kris Jenner

    Kris juxtaposed her all-white outfit with stunning matching emerald rings and earrings from Lorraine Schwartz.

    Demi Lovato

    Demi oozed glamour in a De Beers double-stranded tennis-style necklace.

    Amelia Grey

    Lisa Rinna's daughter was going for gold in a Messika structured necklace with a showstopping square-cut leather 35-carat yellow diamond.

    Emily Ratajkowski

    Emily amped up the glamour of her dazzling vintage Versace dress with Chopard diamante icicle-shaped drop earrings.

    Ayo Edebiri

    Ayo paired her spring-fuelled Loewe gown with resplendent blue dangle earrings from Boucheron jewellery.

    Sarah Jessica Parker

    In a look that oozed Carrie Bradshaw vibes, SJP donned art deco-esque diamond earrings from Bryony Raymond, paired with a dramatic pearl necklace.

    Zendaya

    Zendaya perfectly complimented her Maison Margiela look with emerald green stud earrings and a matching cocktail ring from Bulgari.

    Jennifer Lopez

    JLo did not skimp on the glamour this year. She amped up her striking Schiaparelli couture outfit with a thorn-style diamond necklace and three sets of gold and diamond drop earrings from Tiffany & Co.

    Lily James

    Lily dazzled in a pearlescent Erdem dress which she paired with dazzling diamond ear cuffs and a sculpted ring featuring a pink diamond from Boucheron.

    Emma Chamberlain

    Emma oozed vintage glamour in wearing a Cartier High Jewellery baroque-esque statement choker with dangling earrings.

    Anna Wintour

    Event matriarch Anna dripped in diamonds wearing a statement vintage floral diamond necklace with a pendant, paired with a large vintage floral brooch from S.J. Philips on her Loewe ensemble.

    Ashley Graham

    The British model paired her black mesh sequin gown with dangle diamond earrings and diamond rings from Jared.

    Alexandra Michler Kopelman

    Alexandra adhered to the garden theme with a stunning sapphire blue beaded necklace complete with a statement flower from Irene Neuwirth.

    Rachel Smith

    Rachel Smith oozed sophisticated glamour in lantern-shaped drop earrings paired with a dainty pendant necklace.
