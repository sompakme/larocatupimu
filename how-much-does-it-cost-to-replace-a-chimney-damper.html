How much does it cost to replace a chimney damper?

Answer

A damper door replacement just, with DIY installation, will cost you around $60 on average to repair or replace the damper in your fireplace or chimney. If the frame has to be replaced as well, expect to spend $350-$400 and hire a professional to do the work.

The dampers on fireplaces are located practically exactly above the heat of the fire. If a fracture develops in the damper when it is being quickly heated and cooled, it will no longer function effectively. It is feasible to change a fireplace damper on your own, and you may save a significant amount of money by forsaking the services of a professional.
