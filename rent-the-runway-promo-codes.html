FAQs

Is there a Rent the Runway free shipping option?

Rent the Runway has free shipping for shoppers already signed up for a membership. If you’re ordering without a subscription, then standard shipping starts from $9.95.  

When are the Rent the Runway sales?

You can save on designer clothing at the Rent the Runway sales throughout the year. Simply click through to the Rent the Runway website and select ‘Clearance’ from the menu bar. You can save up to 60% off selected designer styles, including occasion dresses. You can also save on your favorite products with an RtR promo code.

How can I get a Rent the Runway new customer discount?

Rent The Runway discount codes are common for new customers. Currently, you can browse this Marie Claire page for 40% off new membership plans. Simply copy your RtR promo code and paste it at the checkout. 

Is there a Rent the Runway student discount?

Rent the Runway does not have any student discount, as of writing. However, our research showed they are on the Student Beans website and have previously had student discounts. Make sure to check this page for any updates or the Rent the Runway website for any updates and other RtR coupon codes.

Does Rent the Runway have a loyalty program?

No. Rent the Runway previously had an RTR loyalty program in 2021, although this appears not to be running anymore. You can still sign up for a Rent the Runway plan. This has free shipping included in the price, over 750 designed brands, and 3 plans to choose from. 

What is the Rent the Runway return policy?

Rent the Runway has separate return policies for rentals or products you have bought. Rental clothing can be returned at any time, and they should be packed in their original garment bag, with any dust jackets, boxes, or plastic. Any purchases - aside from the final sale - can be returned within 7 days of delivery. However, there is a $5.95 restocking fee plus tax.
