Plotlines

The Tyrant Wants to Live Honestly is a fantasy romance manhwa that revolves around the themes of reincarnation, karma, fate, and love. The main characters are Dorothea and Theon, who have a tragic history in their past lives but are given a second chance in a new world.

Dorothea was the tyrant of the Ubera Empire, who ruled with an iron fist and oppressed the people. She was in love with Theon, the hero of the rebellion, who pretended to be her loyal knight. However, Theon betrayed her and stabbed her in the heart, ending her reign of terror. Theon was the hero of the rebellion, who fought against the tyranny of Dorothea.
