Boutique Hotel t Fraeyhuis Review, Bruges, Belgium

Bruges, Belgium

This hotel provided a welcome addition to the already formidable choice of lovely places to stay in Bruges when it opened in 2019. It's set in a beautifully converted, centuries-old manor house, and offers not only first-class accommodation but also a privileged position next to the Minnewater Park and lake.
