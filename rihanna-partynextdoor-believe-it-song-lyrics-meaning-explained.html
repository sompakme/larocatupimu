Rihanna, PARTYNEXTDOOR Believe It Song Lyrics Meaning Explained

  • R&B singer PARTYNEXTDOOR released a new song with Rihanna, called "Believe It," today.
  • This marks one of Rih's latest collaborations as fans await her ninth album.

Amid building her fashion and beauty empire and donating $5 million to coronavirus relief efforts, Rihanna treated fans with a tiny taste of new music.

The superstar lends her vocals to PARTYNEXTDOOR's new track, "Believe It," which appears on his new album PARTYMOBILE that dropped at midnight. Rih takes the choruses in the song, singing from the perspective of a skeptical woman wronged by a partner who's now asking for her forgiveness. Though this isn't quite the full Rihanna album her fans—and Drake—have been waiting for, "Believe It" is still worth celebrating.

Rihanna last album, Anti, arrived in 2016. Since then, the singer has hopped on a couple collaborations as the world anticipates her ninth LP, including DJ Khaled's "Wild Thoughts" and N.E.R.D.'s "Lemon" in 2017. Her next album wasn't released in 2019 as expected, but Rih has teased that the project is indeed in the works.

In the meantime, she'll hold us over with "Believe It." Listen to the track above, and read through the full lyrics below.
