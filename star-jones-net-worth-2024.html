Star Jones’ Early Life and Career Beginnings

Star Jones was born on March 24, 1962, in Badin, North Carolina. She graduated from the University of Houston Law Center in 1986 and started her career as a prosecutor in the Kings County District Attorney’s Office in Brooklyn, New York. Her legal prowess and charismatic personality soon caught the attention of Court TV, where she served as a legal correspondent, covering high-profile cases.

Breakthrough in Television

Star Jones’ breakthrough came when she was selected as one of the original co-hosts of the ABC daytime talk show “The View” in 1997. Her tenure on the show, which lasted nine years, significantly increased her visibility and contributed to her growing net worth.

Authoring and Fashion Designing

Star Jones has also authored three books, further diversifying her income sources. Her books, “You Have to Stand for Something, or You’ll Fall for Anything,” “Shine… A Physical, Emotional, and Spiritual Journey to Finding Love,” and “Satan’s Sisters,” have all been well-received. Additionally, she launched her own fashion line, “Status,” in 2008, which caters to full-figured women.

Advocacy and Philanthropy

Star Jones is a passionate advocate for heart health and women’s empowerment. She founded the National Association of Professional Women (NAPW) and the American Heart Association’s Go Red for Women movement. Her philanthropic efforts have not only raised her profile but also added to her overall wealth.

Star Jones’ Net Worth Growth Over the Years

Star Jones’ net worth has seen a steady increase over the years. In 2000, her net worth was estimated at $2 million. By 2010, it had grown to $10 million, thanks to her successful career in television, authoring, and fashion designing. As of 2024, her net worth is projected to be around $20 million.

Income Sources

Real Estate Investments

Star Jones has made some savvy real estate investments over the years. She owns a luxury apartment in New York City and a mansion in New Jersey. These properties significantly contribute to her net worth.

Endorsements and Partnerships

Star Jones has also earned from endorsements and partnerships with brands like Payless and Kohl’s. These deals have added to her wealth and increased her visibility.

Legal Career

Conclusion

Star Jones’ projected net worth in 2024 of $20 million is a testament to her diverse career and savvy business decisions.
