Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson and girlfriend Lauren Hashian welcome baby girl

    Christmas has come early for Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson and his girlfriend Lauren Hashian. The couple have welcomed their first child together, sources have confirmed to HELLO!

    Lauren gave birth to a healthy baby girl on Wednesday, just days after she revealed how much she was enjoying her pregnancy in a sweet Instagram post. Sharing a photo of herself and the actor together as she proudly showed off her baby bump, Lauren wrote: "I know I'm late to the party… Sometimes life gets full and busy in the best ways :)… So grateful and in awe of this time.

    Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson and Lauren Hashian have welcomed a baby girl

    "To experience creating a little life is just incredible. This beautiful cycle of life and womanhood beginning again."

    The couple, who have been together nine years, announced they were expecting their first child together in September. Dwayne excitedly revealed that they were expecting a baby girl in an Instagram post at the beginning of November.

    The 43-year-old, who is already a father to 14-year-old daughter Simone, wrote: "I was raised by and live with amazing and strong women, so the universe felt we needed one more... IT'S A BABY GIRL!! THANK YOU guys so much for the awesome support and love you've sent @laurenhashianofficial and myself from around the world. We're so grateful for this blessing and thrilled to share the news with y'all..."

    It seems the former professional wrestler has a "solid" home life and told magazine that this is the root to his success, saying: "We do these stories and we talk so much about the business end, the success end, but then Lauren isn't mentioned, and my daughter is mentioned," he commented. "I always like making sure we find the balance and my home life is in there, and Lauren Hashian is in there, and my daughter is in there.

    "You gotta get the better half in there!" he added. "With all the… success that I've been lucky enough to get? That doesn't happen unless the home life is solid."
