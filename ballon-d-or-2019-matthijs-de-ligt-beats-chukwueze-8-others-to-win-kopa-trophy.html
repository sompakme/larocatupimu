Ballon d'Or 2019: Matthijs de Ligt beats Chukwueze, 8 others to win Kopa trophy

- Ballon d'Or 2019 awards is currently ongoing in France

- Juventus star Matthijs de Ligt wins Kopa 2019 trophy

- The Dutchman becomes the second player after Mbappe to win the prize

Juventus defender Matthijs de Ligt has been crowned the winner of the 2019 edition of the Kopa trophy during the Ballon d'Or awards in France.

The Dutchman beats off competition from Nigerian winger Samuel Chukwueze, Joao Felix of Atletico Madrid, Jadon Sancho and six others to clinch the prestigious award.

He becomes the second youngster to win the prize after Paris Saint Germain forward Kylian Mbappe, who won the maiden edition last December.

The 20-year-old joined the Old Lady in a five-year deal valued around €85.50 million deal in the summer from Ajax Amsterdam.
