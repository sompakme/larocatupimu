  • Jeff Leach is an American Football Coach, Manager, Social Media Personality, and YouTuber’s partner from San Antonia, Texas. He is best known as the loving husband of Brittani Boren Leach, an American Content Creator, YouTube Star, and Instagram Influencer.
  • Brittani gained fame on Instagram by sharing glimpses of her personal lifestyle. By December 2019, she had amassed over 857,000 followers on the platform. She also runs a YouTube channel with a dedicated following of 155k+ subscribers.

Personal Life, Spouse Brittani Boren Leach & Children

  • Prior to his marriage to Brittani Boren Leach, Jeff was previously married and had a daughter named Sydney Leach. Unfortunately, the relationship ended in divorce.
  • Jeff and Brittani, who had both gone through separations, found each other on the online dating website “Match.” They embarked on a romantic journey and tied the knot in October 201The wedding was an intimate affair, attended only by close friends and family.
  • Together, Jeff and Brittani have three sons: Cooper Leach, Crew Leach, and Cash Leach. Tragically, their youngest son, Crew, passed away at the age of three months on December 30, 2019.
  • On December 25, 2019, Brittani put her son down for a nap at a relative’s home, only to discover that he had stopped breathing. She rushed him to the hospital, but sadly, he could not be saved.
