How Far Apart Are Maui and Honolulu?

Traveling to Hawaii is a dream for many people, and once you’re there, the islands themselves are spread out and offer totally different experiences. Two of the most popular islands, Maui and Oahu (home to Honolulu), are separated by approximately 100 miles of the Pacific Ocean.

The shortest distance between Maui and Honolulu is from Kahului Airport on Maui to Daniel K. Inouye International Airport in Honolulu, which is about 97 miles. If you’re traveling by plane, it’s a quick 30-minute flight between the two islands. If you prefer to take a ferry, that’s also an option, but it’s a longer journey. The only ferry service between Maui and Oahu departs from Lahaina Harbor and arrives at the Aloha Tower Marketplace in Honolulu. This journey takes about 3 hours.

FAQs About the Distance Between Maui and Honolulu

How far is the Maui to Oahu ferry?

The Maui to Oahu ferry is a popular option for those who want to travel between the islands. The ferry service operates between Lahaina Harbor on Maui and the Aloha Tower Marketplace in Honolulu. The ferry journey takes approximately 3 hours, providing travelers with a scenic and leisurely way to make the crossing.

Is there a bridge from Maui to Oahu?

There is no bridge connecting Maui and Oahu. The islands are separated by approximately 100 miles of ocean, and the only modes of transportation between them are by air or sea. While there have been discussions about potential bridge projects in the past, the logistics and environmental impact of such a structure have made it a challenging and unlikely endeavor.

What is the best way to travel between Maui and Honolulu?

The best way to travel between Maui and Honolulu depends on your preferences and schedule. If you’re looking for a quick and convenient option, flying between the islands is the most efficient choice. It’s a short 30-minute flight from Kahului Airport in Maui to Daniel K. Inouye International Airport in Honolulu. If you’re more interested in a scenic journey and have time to spare, taking the ferry from Lahaina Harbor to the Aloha Tower Marketplace is a relaxing and enjoyable alternative.
