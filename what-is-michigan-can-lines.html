The history of Michigan Can Lines is a tale of American ingenuity and a whole lot of elbow grease. Founded in 1942 by a guy named Philbert "The Can-do Man" Cannoli (yes, really!), the company started out making simple tin cans for the war effort. But Philbert wasn't content with just churning out basic containers. He dreamt of cans that could do more, cans that could conquer any challenge.

And conquer they did! Over the years, Michigan Can Lines has evolved into a major player in the industrial can market. Their products are used in everything from construction to food processing, and their reputation for quality is legendary.
