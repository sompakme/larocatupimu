What kind of magnet will stick to stainless steel?

How do you make steel magnetic?

Stroke the magnet along half the steel, repeatedly.

Stick the magnet anywhere on the steel about halfway along its length, then drag it to one tip. Repeat several times, stroking only in one direction, and only along half of the steel. The more you do this, the more magnetized the steel will become.
