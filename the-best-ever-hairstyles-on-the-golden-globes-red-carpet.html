The best ever hairstyles on the Golden Globes red carpet

    Every year the world's most glamorous women take to the Golden Globes red carpet, pulling off all the stops with dazzling dresses. However, it's not just their fashion sense that turns heads – every year the stars continue to give us major hair envy with their striking hairstyles. We take a look back at the best hairdos from the glitzy awards – click through for the full list…

    Emily Blunt in 2015

    The brunette beauty channelled a Grecian goddess when she stepped out with an eye-catching plaited hairstyle. The star opted for a middle parting with two braids on either side, worked down towards a low braided bun for a glamorous finish.

    Jennifer Aniston in 2010

    The former star gave us major hair envy when she stepped out with her blonde locks worn down in tousled waves parted to the side to enhance the chic layered effect – to recreate the look simply brush through your locks and add a touch of texture spray.

    Charlize Theron in 2012

    Charlize demonstrated the power of a great hair accessory, adding a touch of sparkle to her gorgeous curls with a silver dazzling headband.

    Angelina Jolie in 2012

    The t actress was the epitome of elegance as she arrived for the ceremony with husband Brad Pitt. We love the chic twisted bun she chose for the night - a simple and striking red carpet classic.

    Jessica Alba in 2013

    The Californian beauty channelled old Hollywood for the ceremony, complementing her pale pink strapless gown by leaving her glossy tresses worn down in loose retro curls, swept to the side for a classic glamorous finish.

    Anne Hathaway in 2006

    The star showed off her beauty credentials with an edgy braided style with plaited detailing along her hairline. For a quick fix way to add volume and ensure it holds all night, follow Anne's lead and add a thin headband closely matching your hair colour and push back at the roots.

    Beyoncé in 2009

    Thehitmaker took the ponytail from day to night with opting for a poker straight and sleek style, with a knotted detail at the nape of her neck. Add a touch of glamour to your own hairstyle by following in the star's footsteps and opting for a dramatic side parting.

    Scarlett Johansson in 2005

    The star was the ultimate blonde bombshell, channelling Marilyn Monroe with her platinum hued locks styled into thick, voluminous curls. The star complemented her 'do with a slick of dark pink glossy lipstick.

    Kate Winslet in 2012

    Kate wowed on the red carpet with her hair styled into a low chignon, her blonde tresses left wavy and swept to the side for a laidback touch. It proved to be an exciting night for the star, who picked up the trophy for Best Actress for her performance in.

    Eva Mendes in 2009

    Eva not only topped best-dressed lists around the world for her elegant gown, but the star's eye-catching Brigitte Bardot-inspired bouffant hairstyle also firmly established her as a modern day beauty icon.

    Cate Blanchett in 2014

    All eyes were on Cate as she stepped on the red carpet in a striking black lace gown, and her beauty look was equally as striking. The actress, who picked up the Best Actress award for her role in , wore her blonde locks in a sleek and smooth faux bob style, tucked behind her ears to show off her sparkling statement earrings.

    Kate Hudson in 2010

    Sleek on one side and heaps of volume on the other formed Kate's striking hairstyle for the red carpet. We love the textured finish to her look, and it's super easy to recreate – simply backcomb through your locks with your fingers.

    Lea Michele in 2011

    We were big fans of Lea's showstopping wavy updo, matching her pretty pink gown. The former star left strands loose for a laidback vibe, and complemented the 'do with a slick of pale pink lipgloss.

    Julia Roberts in 2014

    The star looked every inch a beauty icon with her glossy brunette locks styled into a voluminous Bardot-esque updo, paired with dramatic smokey eye make-up for a showstopping red carpet look.

    Katie Holmes in 2015

    Katie took the super sleek ponytail to the next level, showing off the heaps of length with a slick low ponytail and worn to the side for that extra wow factor.
