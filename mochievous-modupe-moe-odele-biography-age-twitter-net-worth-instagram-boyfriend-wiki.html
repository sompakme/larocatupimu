Biography

Modupe Odele (born 3 November 1989) known professionally as Mochievous Moe (Moe Odele) is a famous Nigerian social media influencer, lawyer and the founder of ScaleMyHustle and Vazi Legal.

She became a leading force in the campaign which push for the disbandment of the Special Anti-Robbery Squad (SARS).
She and other legal firms provided legal aids to the peaceful protesters illegally arrested during the October 2020 nationwide protest in Nigeria.
