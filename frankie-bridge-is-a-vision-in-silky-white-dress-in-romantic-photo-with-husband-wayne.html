Frankie Bridge is a vision in silky white dress in romantic photo with husband Wayne

The Loose Women star is on a family holiday in the Maldives

    Frankie Bridge made sure to enjoy some one-on-one time with her husband Wayne during their family holiday in the Maldives on Friday.

    The star looked gorgeous in a silky white dress as she cuddled her other half for a late-night photo beside the sea. Frankie's stunning frock hugged her toned figure and she looked picture-perfect as she wrapped her arm around Wayne's waist. The Loose Women star radiated beauty in her makeup-free appearance and wore her sun-kissed hair down in natural waves.

    Others fell in love with Frankie's dress, with many quizzing her on where they can find it for themselves and branding it "dress goals" and "gorgeous".

    Frankie looked gorgeous in her silky dress

    Fans may be out of luck though as the former singer revealed in a recent video that she made a conscious effort to pack old clothes instead of buying anything new for her holiday.
