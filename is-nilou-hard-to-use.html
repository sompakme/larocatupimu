Is Nilou hard to use?

Nilou is not considered a difficult character to use in Genshin Impact. She is relatively easy to build and her playstyle is straightforward. However, to fully maximize her potential, some understanding of her mechanics and team composition is necessary.

Is Nilou easy to build?

Yes, Nilou is easy to build. Stack as much HP on her as you can and focus on increasing her Elemental Mastery and Energy Recharge.

Is Nilou worth to pull for?

It is recommended to pull for Nilou if you already own Primordial Jade Cutter or want to get Key of Khaj-Nisut. Her unique gameplay and high damage output make her a valuable addition to any team.

Is Nilou a good character?

Nilou is a good DPS character but niche. She works best with a team that consists of dendro or hydro characters for optimal playstyle.

Is Nilou a good 5-star?

Yes, Nilou is a good 5-star character. She excels in a hybrid main and sub DPS role and her special Dendro Cores can deal insane damage when scaled off her high HP.

Who is better Nilou or Childe?

In terms of overall damage output, Childe is considered a better option compared to Nilou. Childe’s versatility and stronger abilities make him a more powerful DPS character.

Which sword is best for Nilou?

The Key of Khaj-Nisut is the best sword for Nilou as it provides an HP percentage buff as its main stat and has an effect that further increases her HP.

Who should I pair Nilou with?

When building a team with Nilou, it is important to have a healer as her gameplay revolves around the Bloom Reaction. Additionally, she works well with Hydro or Dendro main DPS characters, a Hydro sub-DPS, and a Dendro healer.

Is Nilou a healer or DPS?

Nilou is primarily a DPS character. Her skills and burst scale with her max HP, making her a strong damage dealer.

Who is better Nilou or Ayato?

Nilou and Ayato have different playstyles and abilities. Ayato is generally easier to play and offers a more balanced kit. However, Nilou can excel in a Bloom composition with the right team setup.

Is ATK useless on Nilou?

ATK is not useful for Nilou as her skills and burst scale purely with her max HP. It is more beneficial to focus on increasing her HP and Elemental Mastery instead.

What set is best on Nilou?

The best artifact set for Nilou is 2-piece Tenacity of the Millelith + 2-piece Vourukasha’s Glow. This set maximizes her HP and provides additional benefits for her playstyle.

Is Nilou or Albedo better?

Nilou and Albedo have different roles in a team composition. Nilou excels in producing high damage output with her unique gameplay mechanics, while Albedo offers strong support and utility. The better choice depends on the specific needs of your team.

Is Nilou only good with Dendro?

While Nilou can be played without dendro characters, she is generally weaker than other Hydro units in that case. Her bloom damage requires a significant investment, making her best utilized with a team consisting of dendro and hydro characters.

What are the cons of Nilou Genshin?

One con of Nilou is that she is unable to perform Charged Attacks while under the effect of certain abilities. These effects are removed once she leaves the field.

Who is better Nilou or Xingqiu?

Nilou is superior to Xingqiu when building around her Bountiful Cores and the Bloom Reaction. However, Xingqiu is generally considered a better Hydro sub-DPS and offers more versatility and damage potential.

How do you maximize Nilou damage?

To maximize Nilou’s damage, prioritize increasing her max HP. It is also important to focus on increasing her Elemental Mastery and Energy Recharge. Additionally, using the Key of Khaj-Nisut can further enhance her damage potential.

Who are the top 3 DPS in Genshin?

The top 3 DPS characters in Genshin Impact are Lyney, Neuvillette, and Ganyu. These characters offer powerful elemental abilities and are highly sought after for their damage output.

Who is the strongest DPS in Genshin?

The strongest Physical DPS character in Genshin Impact is Eula. She excels in dealing physical damage through her Normal and Charged Attacks, making her the top choice in this category.

Who is Nilou mostly shipped with?

Nilou is mostly shipped with Cyno, with Cynoari being the most popular ship. Cylou and Cytham are also shipping options for Nilou.

Does Nilou need crit damage?

If you are using Nilou as a DPS character, then it is recommended to prioritize increasing her max HP
